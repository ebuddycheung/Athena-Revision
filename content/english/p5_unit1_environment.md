# Primary 5 English - Unit 1: Our Environment

## 📖 Reading Text

### The Green School

Our school is called Green Primary School. We care about the environment very much. Every student knows that we should reduce, reuse, and recycle.

At school, we have many green practices. We use both sides of our paper. We bring our own water bottles. We separate our rubbish into different bins. We also have a garden where we grow vegetables.

Last month, our school won the "Green School Award". We were very happy and proud. Our principal said, "Protecting the environment is everyone's responsibility."

We will continue to work hard to keep our school and our Earth clean and beautiful.

## 📚 Grammar Focus

### 1. Present Simple Tense
- **Affirmative**: We **care** about the environment.
- **Negative**: We **do not (don't)** waste paper.
- **Question**: **Do** you recycle?

### 2. Modal Verb "Should"
- **Structure**: Subject + should + verb (base form)
- **Examples**:
  - We **should** reduce waste.
  - You **should** bring your own bottle.

### 3. Conjunctions: Because, So, But
- We are happy **because** we won. (原因)
- We are tired, **but** we are happy. (轉折)
- We use less plastic, **so** we help the Earth. (結果)

## ✏️ Writing Practice

### Sentence Patterns

1. **We should + verb (base form)**
   - We should save water.
   - We should plant more trees.

2. **Because + clause**
   - We are happy because we won the award.
   - We recycle because we want to help the Earth.
